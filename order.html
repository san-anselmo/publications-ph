How to Order

Every purchase supports independent Filipino publishing

Explore our collection of exceptional Philippine literature, poetry, history, and socio-political works. Every purchase directly supports independent publishing in the Philippines.

Print Editions

Browse our catalog and add books to your shopping cart. Standard checkouts generate an order slip to complete via Messenger.

Digital eBooks

Look for the "Buy Digital Edition" button on eligible titles. This lets you purchase and download the PDF/EPUB version instantly via Gumroad.

We offer multiple convenient and secure payment options. Select your preferred channel during checkout.

GCash Wallet

Instant and fee-free transfers. Scan our corporate merchant QR code or transfer directly to our registered number to be sent privately.

Bank Transfer

Direct deposits or secure online transfers to our BPI or BDO corporate accounts.

Cash on Delivery

Pay cash directly to our partner couriers upon doorstep delivery. Nationwide coverage with no upfront fees required.
