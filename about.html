About Us

Preserving the Filipino Intellect

San Anselmo Publications, Inc. is a Philippine-based independent publisher of books, journals, pamphlets, and audiobooks. We are known for our focus on local literature, arts, and socio-political themes.

We actively participate in book fairs and collaborate with academic institutions to promote critical thinking and cultural appreciation across the nation.

Our Mission

We believe that independent publishing is an act of cultural stewardship. By giving voice to Filipino writers, poets, and artists, we aim to document our collective history and shape a more reflective society.

Santelmo: Liwanag sa Dilim

Our flagship literary journal, published quarterly, serves as a vital platform for creative expression. Named after the legendary St. Elmo's fire, it symbolizes illumination, resilience, and the enduring flame of the Filipino spirit.
